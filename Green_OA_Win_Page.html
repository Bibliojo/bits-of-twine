Green Open Access FTW!

You publish in your target journal and upload the Accepted Manuscript (AAM) to your institution’s repository. It’s free, legal, and discoverable! 🎉

Here's how:

  • 📥 Deposit your AAM in Western Scholaris, our institutional repository.
  • 🧭 Not sure what version you can share? Try ShareYourPaper.org—it checks publisher policies for you.
  • 📖 Look up journal policies using Jisc's Open Policy Finder or check the journal’s website under 'self-archiving'.
  • 💌 Still confused? Just email rsclib@uwo.ca and we’ll help you sort it out.
